About LB Networks

Our culture and experience are the drivers of our philosophy. Customers hire us to implement a solution, not sell them hardware!
Our services offer service providers a competitive advantage, which in turn drives business in the fast growing networking segments of mobile backhaul, Ethernet business services and Cloud services. Our network engineers save customers money and ensure delivery of IP transport.

Our Mission

The LB Networks mission is to ensure carrier and enterprise customers have every tool available to optimize their networks. Our solutions are having a transformative impact on our industry. Thanks to our service provider background, we have catapulted to the forefront of SLA monitoring and network solutions.

SaaS Applications

OcularIP is the industry’s leading performance monitoring and SLA assurance portal, providing real time, end-to-end visibility with a simple dashboard view of circuit performance and a first-ever web app for total portability.
OcularIP allows service providers to showcase their network performance, thereby driving carrier revenue and improving customer retention.
OcularIP is currently the platform of choice of top ten service providers as well as enterprises and technology manufacturers. OcularIp now supports over 100 technologies with its open standards-based architecture.
Through our purpose-built SaaS application, W.O.O.D., (Workflow Operations Optimization Database), clients can now manage network deployments through a real-time portal, showcasing everything from project milestones and reports on site readiness to automated switch configuration customized by site, all with the click of a mouse.

Engineering Services

We design, stage, test and implement custom, scalable networks. We handle deployments, upgrades and expansions across the transport, service and application layers of the network.
Our Professional Services team members are intimately familiar with tier-one carrier, OEM and industry standards, specifications and procedures. Local Backhaul Networks’ Professional Services Division has deployed technology at over 10,000 carrier facilities throughout the US.
Our network integration capabilities help us seamlessly turn-up and deploy new technologies and services in live wireless, access, transport and backhaul networks.
Our integration team’s knowledge and experience includes wireless and wireline networks covering all phases of network deployments including site audits, staging, final network “as-built” designs, circuit characterization testing and final documentation.
The LB Networks team features certified program management professionals to oversee the process including project planning, design, engineering, implementation and customer acceptance. We provide technical support for FOAs, trials, trouble-ticket resolution and other development or product-related requirements common to complex, multi-vendor environments.

Network Consulting

LB Network’s network consulting facilitates a smooth, seamless experience for clients as their communications platforms migrate to IP-enabled networks. We provide operational and technical expertise, ensuring a safe, reliable transition and a smooth deployment. These services also include the Integration of the OcularIP SLA Assurance platform where applicable.
As part of the LBN consulting services, our consultants can perform a needs assessment and feasibility analysis that examines the current technology environment and incorporates it into our customer’s vision for the future.
Our network strategy and planning experts work with our customers to develop a roadmap for a flexible and dynamic IP-enabled, next generation network.
LB Network’s procurement support services alleviate the pressures that clients face when they develop and evaluate requests for proposals from network suppliers, system vendors and service providers because we are vendor-neutral.

Network/Systems Integration

Our systems integration expertise means we can develop a plan specific to a customer’s strategic needs and design a flexible, robust network infrastructure that enables convergence of all existing applications.
