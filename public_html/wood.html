W.O.O.D.

W.O.O.D is an exclusive, web-based SaaS application developed specifically to track and control network projects. Whether your task is an update, upgrade or expansion, or just the simple need to track assets, change generally involves hundreds if not thousands of variables across a network.

Work-Flow Operations Optimization Database

Major carriers, service providers and other enterprises are using W.O.O.D. with unqualified success because of the capability to capture so many variables.
With W.O.O.D., operators can easily control assets and procedures to reduce costs, achieve timelines and pinpoint issues.
With the click of a mouse, this purpose built database offers instant access to critical information for:

  • Project Milestones
  • Location Information
  • Power Systems
  • Frame Layouts
  • Engineering Documents
  • Technologies Installed
  • Configurations
  • Photo Documentation
  • Test Capabilities/Results
  • Ad Hoc Reporting

The query tool, which is one of the more helpful special features, offers data-specific insight into hundreds of variables by site, by market or by project.
Immediate access to right-time information for such variables as power, technology and space limitations means project control and cost effectiveness.
